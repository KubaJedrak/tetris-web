The goal of the game is to keep clearing the grid for as long as possible by filling rows with tetrominoes. Once filled, a row will empty, the remaining tetrominoes will "fall" and you will gain score. You can do the following:

- rotate the tetromino by pressing Up Arrow,
- move left (by pressing Left Arrow) or right (by pressing Right Arrow),
- move down by one row by pressing Down Arrow,
- Space button starts a new game or pauses/unpauses a game in progress,
- Esc button stops the game

As time goes on, the game will become more difficult, but the amount of points gained will also increase

Good luck!
